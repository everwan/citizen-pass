US Citizenship Test Pass is a study app for the U.S. civics test. This page explains what information the app may use, how that information may be handled, and how to contact us with privacy questions.

ENGLISH

What this app does

US Citizenship Test Pass helps users prepare for the U.S. civics test with USCIS 2008 and 2025 question banks, bilingual practice, mock tests, audio playback, pronunciation recording, premium study features, and study resources.

What information may be used

  • Learning activity such as answer history, saved questions, mistakes, mock test results, and local study preferences
  • Device or app usage information needed for app stability, diagnostics, troubleshooting, or content delivery
  • Advertising-related information when ads are shown inside the free version of the app
  • Purchase or subscription status when premium features are offered
  • Microphone access only if the user chooses pronunciation recording

How the information is used

  • To save study progress and improve review features
  • To support audio playback and pronunciation practice
  • To refresh supported study content when remote updates are enabled
  • To understand app performance and fix bugs
  • To display ads in the free version of the app
  • To manage premium access and restore purchases

Local storage

Study data such as attempts, saved questions, mistakes, mock test results, and preferences are primarily stored on the device.

Third-party services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, consent collection, purchases, diagnostics, analytics, or content delivery. Those services may process device identifiers, purchase status, or technical usage data under their own policies.

Advertising and tracking

The free version of the app may display ads. The current iOS release is not intended to request App Tracking Transparency permission for cross-app tracking.

Your choices

You can choose whether to use the pronunciation recording feature. Depending on your region and device settings, you may also see privacy or advertising consent prompts.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: app.support.us@gmail.com

简体中文

这个应用是做什么的

美国入籍考试通是一款帮助用户准备美国公民入籍 civics test 的学习应用，支持 USCIS 2008 与 2025 版题库、双语练习、模拟考试、音频朗读、跟读录音、会员学习功能和学习资料。

可能涉及的信息

  • 学习记录，例如答题历史、收藏题、错题、模拟考试结果以及本地学习偏好设置
  • 用于稳定性、诊断、问题排查或内容分发的设备或应用使用信息
  • 在免费版应用内展示广告时可能涉及的广告相关信息
  • 在提供会员功能时涉及的购买或订阅状态信息
  • 仅在用户主动使用跟读录音功能时涉及的麦克风权限

这些信息如何使用

  • 用于保存学习进度并优化复习功能
  • 用于支持音频播放和跟读练习
  • 用于在启用远程更新时刷新支持的学习内容
  • 用于了解产品表现并修复问题
  • 用于在免费版中展示广告
  • 用于管理会员权限和恢复购买

本地存储

学习数据，例如做题记录、收藏题、错题、模拟考试结果和偏好设置，当前主要存储在本地设备中。

第三方服务

应用可能会使用第三方服务来支持广告展示、同意收集、购买管理、崩溃排查、分析统计或内容分发。这些服务可能会依据其自身政策处理设备标识符、购买状态或技术使用数据。

广告与追踪

本应用的免费版本可能展示广告。当前 iOS 发布版本不打算请求 App Tracking Transparency 权限来进行跨 App 追踪。

你的选择

你可以自行决定是否启用跟读录音功能。根据你的设备设置或所在地区，应用也可能展示与广告或隐私相关的同意提示。
