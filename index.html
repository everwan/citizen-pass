ENGLISH

What this app does

US Citizenship Test Pass helps users prepare for the U.S. civics test with USCIS 2008 and 2025 question banks, bilingual practice, mock tests, audio playback, pronunciation recording, and study resources.

What information may be used

  • Learning activity such as answer history, saved questions, mistakes, and mock test results
  • Device or app usage information needed for app stability, analytics, and troubleshooting
  • Advertising-related information when ads are shown inside the app
  • Microphone access only if the user chooses pronunciation recording

How the information is used

  • To save study progress and improve review features
  • To support audio playback and pronunciation practice
  • To understand app performance and fix bugs
  • To display and measure advertisements

Local storage

Study data such as attempts, saved questions, mistakes, and mock test results are primarily stored on the device.

Third-party services

The app may use third-party services for advertising, consent collection, diagnostics, or analytics. Those services may process device identifiers or technical usage data under their own policies.

Your choices

You can choose whether to use the pronunciation recording feature. Depending on your region and device settings, you may also see privacy or advertising consent prompts.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact: app.support.us@gmail.com

简体中文

这个应用是做什么的

美国入籍考试通是一款帮助用户准备美国公民入籍 civics test 的学习应用，支持 USCIS 2008 与 2025 版题库、双语练习、模拟考试、音频朗读、跟读录音和学习资料。

可能涉及的信息

  • 学习记录，例如答题历史、收藏题、错题和模拟考试结果
  • 用于稳定性、统计和问题排查的设备或应用使用信息
  • 在应用内展示广告时可能涉及的广告相关信息
  • 仅在用户主动使用跟读录音功能时涉及的麦克风权限

这些信息如何使用

  • 用于保存学习进度并优化复习功能
  • 用于支持音频播放和跟读练习
  • 用于了解产品表现并修复问题
  • 用于展示和衡量广告效果

本地存储

学习数据，例如做题记录、收藏题、错题和模拟考试结果，当前主要存储在本地设备中。

第三方服务

应用可能会使用第三方服务来支持广告展示、同意收集、崩溃排查或分析统计。这些服务可能会依据其自身政策处理设备标识符或技术使用数据。

你的选择

你可以自行决定是否启用跟读录音功能。根据你的设备设置或所在地区，应用也可能展示与广告或隐私相关的同意提示。
